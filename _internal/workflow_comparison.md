## Key Takeaways

### Why Multi-Channel Works

1. **Redundancy = Reliability**
   - If email goes to spam, WhatsApp catches them
   - If WhatsApp is muted, SMS gets through
   - If text is ignored, voice creates urgency

2. **Different Channels = Different Contexts**
   - Email = detailed, professional (desktop)
   - WhatsApp = quick, personal (mobile)
   - SMS = urgent, important (mobile)
   - Voice = human, trustworthy (creates rapport)

3. **Frequency Without Annoyance**
   - 7 emails in 3 days = annoying
   - 3 emails + 2 WhatsApp + 1 SMS + 1 call = helpful

4. **Immediate Feedback**
   - Email: Don't know if read until they reply
   - WhatsApp: See "Read" receipts instantly
   - Voice AI: Know outcome in 2 minutes

5. **Qualification at Scale**
   - Voice AI identifies not-interested leads on Day -1
   - Save time by not prepping for unqualified calls
   - Focus manual effort on engaged leads only
