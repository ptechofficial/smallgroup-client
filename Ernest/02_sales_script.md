## THE CASE STUDY (3-5 minutes)

> "Here's what we did. Three steps."

> "First — ASO. We optimized his app store listing. Keywords, description, screenshots. Made sure when people searched related terms, his app actually showed up."

> "Second — we added a ratings prompt at the right moment in the user experience. This is important because ratings and reviews directly affect how the App Store ranks you."

> "Third — and this is the hack — we created a lifetime offer in the app. A premium feature that normally costs money. We made it free for two days. Then we submitted it to AppAdvice — which is a site that features apps that have gone free. It's like a deal site for apps."

> "The result: 106,000 downloads in that window. And here's the real kicker — the download spike boosted his keyword rankings so much that his organic downloads kept growing after. What started as a single campaign became a sustainable growth engine for the business."

> "Now imagine that for your app. You already have real, unique data. Insider trading insights aren't commoditized — most apps don't have what you have. If we run this same playbook alongside the content engine, we're attacking downloads from two directions: the organic content flywheel AND a concentrated download spike that boosts your rankings."

> "That's how we hit 10 to 20K downloads in two months. And the beauty is — the content keeps compounding after. Month 3, Month 4, Month 6 — it only gets bigger."
