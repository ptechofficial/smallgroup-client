## How It Works

### 1. Content Discovery

Apify scrapes TikTok and Instagram daily. Target keywords: "make money online 2026", "no-code business", "sell AI to businesses", "SMMA automation", "dropshipping alternatives." Filters for above-average engagement. Extracts transcriptions, hooks, and metrics.

800+ pieces scraped/month. Only the best become scripts.

### 2. Script Generation

LLM (Claude or GPT-4o) rewrites viral transcripts into Italian scripts built around the Agent Kit 4-step method. Four angles rotate: attack old models, promise freedom, hybrid model, no-code simplicity.

Structure: **Hook → Value → CTA to Free Training.** Captions and hashtags included.

Scripts go to Airtable. Client clicks "Approve Script" before anything moves forward.

### 3. Video Production

Approved script triggers the pipeline:

1. **TTS** — Italian voiceover
2. **AI avatar** — lip-synced to audio
3. **B-roll** — stock footage from Pexels or client archive
4. **Assembly** — final MP4 with subtitles (ArcAds or Higgsfield)

Video saved to Google Drive. Link appears in Airtable. Client clicks "Approve Video" before scheduling.

**Two approval gates. Nothing posts without sign-off.**

#### Voice & Avatar Tools

ElevenLabs is in the PRD, but it may not be the best fit for recreating the reference video style. We'll test 2–3 options during Week 1–2 and let the client pick:

| Tool | Best For |
|------|----------|
| **ElevenLabs** | Italian TTS, voice cloning |
| **HeyGen** | Realistic avatars, lip-sync |
| **Synthesia** | All-in-one avatar + voice |
| **D-ID** | Cost-effective avatars at volume |

Voice and avatar can come from different providers. The stack is modular.

### 4. Distribution

AyrShare API handles posting to Instagram Reels and TikTok. Staggered timing, platform-optimized captions and hashtags. One API for both platforms.

CTA drives to link in bio or keyword comment for ManyChat DM automation.

**Daily posting, both platforms, fully automated.**

### 5. Analytics & Feedback Loop

Tracks every video at 48 hours and 7 days: views, likes, comments, shares, link clicks. Identifies which angles convert — money hook vs. freedom hook, dropshipping attack vs. no-code pitch.

Winning angles get weighted in the next batch of scripts. The system improves every cycle.
