Confirmation Page

This is NOT a throwaway “thanks for booking” page. It’s the main tool for getting prospects to show up informed and ready.

Page Structure

  • Confirmation Video (1-2 min) — short personal video from you welcoming them and setting expectations for the call
  • Breakout Videos Section — 4-8 short videos, each answering ONE common question prospects ask
  • Testimonials — 2-3 student success stories (average results, not outliers)
  • Trust Assets — links to your YouTube channel, Discord community, credibility markers

Breakout Video Topics

  • “How much money do I need to start?”
  • “What if I have no experience?”
  • “How long until I see results?”
  • “What makes Academia Ecom different?”
  • “What does the course actually include?”
  • “Is Amazon FBA still worth it in 2026?”
  • “Meet your sales rep”
  • “What happens on the call?”

We write the scripts. You record 1-2 min each. ~15 min total.

Email Sequences

Two separate sequences: one for people who haven’t booked yet, and one for people who have booked but haven’t had their call yet.

Pre-Booking Emails

For leads who filled the form but haven’t booked

  • Email 1 (immediate) — Deliver the training link
  • Email 2 (Day 2-3) — “Did you finish watching?”
  • Email 3 (Day 5-6) — Student success story
  • Email 4 (Day 8-9) — Standalone value / authority
  • Email 5 (Day 11-12) — Final push + urgency

When they book → automatically exits this sequence and enters post-booking.

Post-Booking Research Emails

For people who booked — sent between booking and call (2-3 days)

  • Email 1 (booking day) — Confirmation + link to research hub
  • Email 2 (same day, PM) — Breakout video: startup capital
  • Email 3 (Day 2, AM) — Student success story
  • Email 4 (Day 2, PM) — “What happens on the call”
  • Email 5 (Day 3, AM) — “What makes AE different”
  • Email 6 (2 hrs before) — Meeting link + reminder

These are NOT reminder spam. Each delivers a breakout video or real value.

Lead Capture Page

A qualifying form that sits before the landing page. Captures contact info from all ad traffic, segments leads by awareness and budget, and fires pixel data to Meta.

6-Step Form Flow

  • Step 1: Name
  • Step 2: Email
  • Step 3: WhatsApp number
  • ↑ After Step 3, we have enough to follow up even if they abandon
  • Step 4: “How much do you know about Amazon FBA?” (awareness tag)
  • Step 5: “How much capital do you have available?” (budget tag)
  • Step 6: “What’s your monthly income goal?” (ambition tag)

What Happens On Submit

  • Contact created in GHL with qualifying tags (awareness:hot, capital:high, etc.)
  • Source tag applied (source:instagram / source:facebook / source:youtube)
  • Redirects to Landing Page
  • Triggers pre-booking email + WhatsApp nudge
  • Meta Pixel fires Lead event
  • Incomplete forms still captured (tagged incomplete-form)

Sales Rep Protocol + Pixel

A documented playbook for your sales reps to follow after someone books, plus advanced Meta pixel setup so your ads get smarter over time.

Sales Rep Pre-Call Protocol

  • Within 2-4 hrs of booking: Rep sends a selfie video via WhatsApp — personal, not scripted. We write the talking points.
  • Day 2 (if no reply): Share a trust asset — a breakout video or student story matched to their qualifying tags
  • Day of call, 1 hr before: Quick check-in message with meeting link
  • Full script templates provided for every touchpoint
  • Rules documented: real WhatsApp number, professional but natural tone, human conversation if they reply

Pixel Conditioning + Conversion API

  • Standard pixel: Lead on form submit, Schedule on booking
  • Conversion API: Sales rep marks “qualified” in GHL pipeline → that signal fires back to Meta
  • Over time, Meta’s algorithm learns to find you better leads — not just people who book, but people who actually qualify
  • Setup and configuration included — we handle the technical integration
