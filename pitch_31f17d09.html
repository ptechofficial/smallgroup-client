1
Pillar One

Content Discovery & Scraping

Find what's already working in the niche — viral hooks, proven angles, high-engagement content — and use it as the blueprint.

Automated Viral Detection

  • Apify scrapes TikTok + Instagram daily
  • Targeted keywords: "make money online 2026", "no-code business", "sell AI to businesses", "SMMA automation"
  • Filters for above-average engagement ratio
  • Extracts transcriptions and hook structures

What Gets Extracted

  • Full video transcription (audio to text)
  • Hook breakdown (first 3-5 seconds)
  • Engagement metrics (views, likes, comments ratio)
  • Content angle classification (money, freedom, simplicity, etc.)

Every video starts from a proven blueprint. No guessing what content will resonate — we reverse-engineer what's already working.

2
Pillar Two

AI Script Generation

Viral references go in. Italian-language scripts for the Agent Kit funnel come out — with the right hooks, angles, and CTAs.

Smart Rewriting

  • LLM (Claude/GPT-4o) rewrites viral transcripts
  • Adapted to the Agent Kit 4-step framework
  • 4 marketing angles: attack old models, promise freedom, hybrid business model, no-code simplicity
  • All output strictly in Italian

Script Structure

  • Hook (first 3 seconds — attention grabber)
  • Value body (4-step method explanation)
  • CTA driving to Free Training
  • Caption copy + hashtags auto-generated

Pillar Three

Dynamic Video Production

Approved scripts become finished videos automatically — AI avatar, Italian voiceover, subtitles, B-roll, ready to post.

Production Pipeline

  • ElevenLabs TTS — energetic Italian voice
  • AI avatar synced to audio (lip-sync)
  • Auto-generated subtitles overlaid
  • Thematic B-roll from Pexels or client archive

Quality Control

  • Finished MP4 saved to Google Drive
  • Link posted to Airtable (no database bloat)
  • "Approve Video" button — client reviews before posting
  • Two human gates: script approval + video approval

  • Consistent daily presence across both platforms

1 post per day, every day, on each platform. Consistent presence without manual effort.
